Heart disease prediction system

Project Aim

The purpose of this project is to predict the heart disease of a patient.
Output ‘1’ will show that the patient has heart disease and ‘0’ will show that the patient don not has heart disease.

input attributes:

cp (Chest Pain Type)
restecg (resting electrocardiographic results)
ca (Number of major vessels)
thal (Thalium stress result)

output attributes:

Target
‘1’ for (The patient has heart disease)
‘0’ (The patient do not has heart disease)
